Welcome to Hailo AI Cam

To use the AI Camera, you must leave this Wi-Fi popup and open the app in a full browser.

🔒 SSL Certificate Warning
Because this is an offline local network, your browser will warn you that the connection is "Not Private" or "Not Safe".

This is completely normal and safe. You must click "Advanced" ➔ "Proceed to hailocam.local" (or 10.42.0.1) to load the app.
📷 Camera Permissions
When the app loads, your browser will ask for permission to use the camera. You must tap Allow for the object detection to work.
Important:

Android: Tapping the button should automatically open Chrome.

iOS (iPhone): Tapping the button will turn the "Cancel" button in the top right into a blue "Done" button. Tap "Done" to close this window, then manually open Safari and type in https://hailocam.local/app.html (or https://10.42.0.1/app.html)
